My Favorite switches

Gazzew Boba u4t Switches(tactile)

-Boba U4T switches are all about producing a nice “thocc” sound on impact.
The Gazzew Boba U4t has a design with a yellow stem and a milky white housing.
It requires 62 grams of actuation force and 4mm travel distance.

C3 Tangerine(linear)

-The C3 tangerine switches are smooth, very smooth.
It is clear to see that C3 is pretty confident about crowning themselves as the smoothest linears in the game.
It has 67g of dark stem (actuation force).

Gateron Oil-King(linear)

-Gateron are popular as switch that don't require to lube and it get better as time pass by.
It is the latest switches on the list , recently become available at early 2022
It has 55 Actuation and 4mm travel.

C3 Banana Split(linear)

-One the of best looking switches which include yellow, purple and pink color and have very good sound and feel.
It is 62g gold spring and it is 5 ping PCB mount type.

NovelKeys x Kailh Cream Switches(linear)

-Just like the name , the cream switch have unique sound of "creamy" ness to it.
It has 55g for force and 5 ping and cosider mid level price linear switch.

Cherry blue switches(Clicky)

-It is the gold standards are clicky switches.
Cherry brands are well establish and their Cherry blue switches are one of the best clicky switches out there.
It has 60g for force and 4mm travel.
